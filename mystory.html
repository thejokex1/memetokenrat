My Story

The name’s Ratatoot. Yeah, I was that degen rat who went all in—sunk every last coin into whatever meme token promised me the moon. Doge? Done. Frog? Absolutely. I was everywhere, chasing gains, watching those numbers climb like I was invincible.

Then it all came crashing down. Rugs, pump-and-dumps, the whole crypto circus left me with nothing. Now I’m living in a cardboard box, broke, bitter, but plotting my grand comeback.

I’m gonna make my own token. RAT. No minting. No inflation. Just straight-up rewards for the loyal. Can I come back? We’ll see. I will keep writing, keep checking frens..

This is an experiment, and just for fun. There is no real value to this project. Do not take it as an investment. Just enjoy it.
